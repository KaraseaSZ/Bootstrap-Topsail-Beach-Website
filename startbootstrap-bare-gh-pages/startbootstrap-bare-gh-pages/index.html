Welcome to Topsail Beach

Join us at Topsail Beach, where the ocean’s rhythm meets community spirit, and every sunset paints a new chapter in our island’s story.

At Topsail Beach, nature’s tranquil beauty unfolds before your eyes. From breathtaking sunrises to spectacular sunsets, this pristine island paradise invites you to escape the ordinary. Nestled along the northernmost 11 miles of Topsail Island, our town boasts sandy beaches, maritime forests, and an abundance of natural treasures. Whether you’re seeking a laid-back retreat or an adventure by the waves, North Topsail Beach promises serenity and discovery.

Wildlife enthusiasts will delight in regular sightings of dolphins, sea turtles, and shorebirds. Keep your eyes peeled for more elusive creatures like bald eagles and the occasional alligator in the backwaters. Along the shore, you can stroll, sunbathe, swim, and search for shells and ancient sharks’ teeth. For sports enthusiasts, fishing opportunities abound—from the ocean pier, beach, or boat. And if you want to explore beyond the shoreline, our town is conveniently located between the cities of Wilmington and Jacksonville.
